Prototype/Systems: AAV-01

Capabilities of AAV-01

The AAV-01 (Amphibious Assault Vehicle - Mark 1) is designed for military applications, capable of operating on both land and water. Key capabilities include:

  • Amphibious operations allowing for beach landings and waterborne missions.
  • Heavy armament for enhanced combat capabilities on the battlefield.
  • Advanced navigation systems for operation in diverse environments.
  • High mobility on rough terrains and maneuverability.
  • Enhanced troop transport capacity.

Defence Applications

The AAV-01 serves critical roles in various defence operations:

  • Quick deployment of infantry troops in combat zones.
  • Providing fire support during amphibious assaults.
  • Logistics support for transporting supplies and equipment to forward positions.
  • Enhancing situational awareness with integrated surveillance systems.
